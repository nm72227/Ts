Our Story

A family recipe,
half a century in the making.

In 1974, Tariq Sahib opened a small mithai counter in Lahore's old city with one goal: to make sweets the way his grandmother taught him — no shortcuts, no artificial colours, no compromise on ghee.

Today, three generations later, that same philosophy still runs every kitchen shift. We source milk from a single trusted dairy in Kasur, grind our own cardamom, and still wrap every gift box by hand.

🥛 Pure ingredients
Real ghee, whole milk, no artificial colours or preservatives.
👐 Made by hand
Every piece is shaped, fried, or baked by our trained halwais.
📦 Fresh daily
We make in small batches twice a day. Yesterday's stock doesn't stay.
🎁 Gift-ready
Beautiful handcrafted boxes for weddings, Eid, and corporate gifting.

Pure ingredients
